THE PRACTICE
Get Going! or a Survival Guide for the Graphic Designer in the age of coming Insurrection.
As a result of investigating how the rebellious text, written by the anonymous group "Invisible Comittee", is connected to the future of Graphic Design. Designed with the open source program, using only open source typefaces and produced manualy.
We live under a system of bureaucratic principles which are extended to every aspect of our existence. What we call "globalization” has nothing to do with the effacement of borders and the free movement of people, products, and ideas, but rather trapping people behind national borders. This project is a model of the world's effective planetary-scale administrative bureaucratic system currently in place.
In collaboration with Rebecca Rui.
Internet, as a disembodied form of communication, often assumed to be impersonal and untrustworthy. “Globalising intimacy” is an installation, visualising how the absence of physical proximity creates intimacy. The installation includes 2 screens, placed in 2 different spots. Each of them shows the same message. However, you can only read the message how it is supposed to be if both you and the person in front of the other screen stands still. If one of you is moving the message is distorted. If one of you is absent, there's a piece of text missing.
In collaboration with Dora Kerekes.
With special thanks to Daniel Shiffman, Edwin Jakobs, Lizzie Malcolm, Boyd Rotgans, Gabor Kerekes.
The interactive installation "Fourth Dimension" is an attampt to comprehence the idea of a 4-dimesional space. Combining 4 different media (visual projection, sound, touch and space) and influencing different sources of perception, the project works as a fluid constraction, reacting on touch, by changing visual and sound aspects.
