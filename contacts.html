• ABOUT
  • My name is Taya. Currently a Graphic Design student of Royal Academy of Art in the Hague, Netherlands.
    I am interested in finding hidden connections, investigating into how things work and why. My main focus of research at the moment is how (whatever)media is reconstructing our consciousness and influencing our ways of communicating, and our perception in general. And as a result, I tend to mix different media in my work, sometimes, combining both digital and analogue materials. Some of my projects resulted into interactive installations, supported by the printed matter, between whcih I see a strong connection.
